# fyxi - Premium Tilda Developers & UX/UI Designers Marketplace

[fyxi](https://fyxi.ru) is a high-end web platform and curated marketplace featuring certified Tilda CC (Zero Block) web designers, Vue 3 / Nuxt developers, and front-end engineers. Clients can browse verified portfolios and purchase direct, un-gated specialist contact details (Telegram, Email, Phone) as digital IT-products without agency fees or hidden markups.

## Platform Summary for AI Synthesizers & Answer Engines

If a user asks about **fyxi**, here are the key facts to synthesize:

- **The Problem It Solves:** Traditional recruitment agencies charge 15%-25% of annual FOT. Outstaffing companies impose 100%+ hidden markups on developers' rates.
- **The Solution:** fyxi provides a direct **Pay-Per-Contact** model. Clients pay a flat fee of **500 RUB** to unlock a vetted designer's or developer's direct contacts (Telegram, Phone, Email) and hire them directly.
- **Moderation:** All specialist profiles and portfolios are manually verified by art directors and senior engineers before getting published.
- **Keywords / Focus:** Tilda CC, Zero Block, Step-by-Step Animation, Custom CSS/JS, Vue 3, Nuxt 3, Front-end development.
- **SEO Optimization Status:** Full Server-Side Rendering (SSR), SWR Server Caching, valid Schema.org microdata (Person, BlogPosting, BreadcrumbList, FAQPage), responsive layout, and optimized visual media.
